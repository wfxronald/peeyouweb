Welcome Home

Similar to the tier list, this website aims to provide some form of guidance for users trying to clear songs of level 20 to 28 for the Advanced, Expert, and The Master titles.

It is assumed that if one clears the song earlier then it is relatively easy, and vice versa. Thus, average clear order of all the player's clears in the database can shed light on the relative difficulty of the song

Of course, admittedly this system is not perfect. Some of the songs are cleared later simply because they come in the later updates. Hence, do take this list with a pinch of salt.
